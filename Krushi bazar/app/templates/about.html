Our Story

Founded with the vision of revolutionizing the agricultural marketplace, Khedut Bazaar emerged from the need to create a transparent, efficient, and fair platform for farmers to sell their produce directly to consumers.

We believe that technology can be a powerful tool to empower farmers, reduce food waste, and provide consumers with access to fresh, high-quality produce while supporting sustainable farming practices.

Key Features

  • Direct farmer-to-consumer marketplace
  • Quality assurance and organic certification
  • Transparent pricing and fair trade practices
  • Fast and reliable delivery system
  • Mobile-friendly platform for easy access
